What We Offer

Complete Eye Care

A full spectrum of vision and eye health services for the whole Bristol community.

👁
NHS Eye Examinations
Fully funded NHS sight tests for eligible patients, with thorough assessments of vision and eye health. Advanced OCT as standard.
🔬
Private Eye Examinations
Advanced diagnostics including retinal imaging and detailed assessment of eye health conditions. Advanced OCT as standard.
🕶
Designer Eyewear
Curated collection from everyday essentials to statement designer pieces, fitted with precision.
💧
Contact Lens Fitting
Full fitting service including complex prescriptions, specialist lenses for dry eye and keratoconus.
👶
Myopia Management
Evidence-based techniques to slow myopia progression and protect long-term vision.
💡
Dry Eye Clinic
Specialist diagnosis and management using the latest diagnostic tools and proven treatment plans.
✨
Lumecca-I (IPL Therapy)
Intense Pulsed Light therapy to reduce vascular inflammation and treat ocular surface disease.
⚡
Forma-I (RF Therapy)
Bipolar Radiofrequency therapy targeting meibomian glands, improving dry eye whilst stimulating collagen.
🏥
Post-Cataract Care
Expert post-operative follow-up and optimisation of vision following cataract surgery.
